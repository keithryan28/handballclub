Tournament June 15, 2023

Summer Championship Results

Our club teams performed exceptionally well in the regional summer championships held last weekend at the City Sports Complex.

The men's team secured first place in Division A after a thrilling final match against City Rivals Club, winning 28-26. Captain Mark Johnson led the team with 9 goals, while goalkeeper Sam Wilson made several crucial saves in the final minutes.

Our women's team also had a strong showing, finishing third in their division. Special mention goes to rookie player Lisa Chen who was named Most Promising Player of the tournament.

The junior teams (U16 and U14) both reached the semifinals in their categories, demonstrating the strength of our youth development program.

Congratulations to all players and coaches for their hard work and dedication!

Program May 28, 2023

Junior Development Program Launch

We're excited to announce our new Junior Development Program for players aged 8-16, starting July 1st.

The program will run every Saturday morning from 9am to 11am and will focus on:

  • Fundamental handball skills
  • Game strategy and tactics
  • Physical conditioning
  • Teamwork and sportsmanship

The program will be led by our head coach, former national team player David Martinez, along with three assistant coaches. Spaces are limited to ensure quality instruction, so early registration is recommended.

Registration fee: $150 for 8 sessions (2 months). Club members receive a 20% discount.

Facility May 10, 2023

New Court Lighting Installed

We're pleased to announce the completion of our court lighting upgrade project. All four courts now feature state-of-the-art LED lighting systems that provide:

  • Brighter, more even illumination
  • 50% energy savings compared to our old system
  • Adjustable brightness levels
  • Flicker-free performance for better visibility

The new lighting was funded through a combination of club reserves and generous donations from our sponsors. Special thanks to BrightLight Solutions for their professional installation.

Members are invited to a special "Light Up the Night" event this Friday from 7-9pm to experience the new lighting system.
