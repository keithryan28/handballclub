About Carrick-on-Suir Handball & Racquetball Club

Celebrating a rich heritage in handball and racquetball, fostering community engagement and sporting excellence.

Club Facilities

The Carrick-on-Suir Handball & Racquetball Club offers a vibrant, community-focused environment with a fully equipped indoor 40x20 court, two outdoor 1-ball 60x30 court, two changing rooms with showers, male and female restrooms, a viewing area, a tea/coffee station, and a referee stand for official matches. A newly added gym is available for all paid members.

Membership Fees: €50 per adult, €20 per juvenile, €60 per family per year. Court and gym usage is on a pay-for-lights basis: €1 per 15 minutes for the court and €1 per 25 minutes for the gym.

Court Bookings:Bookings can be made via the TeamUp app which will be set upon joining the club.

Club History

1970s

The club grounds were first established and became a hub for handball in Carrick-on-Suir.

1985

The current handball and racquetball club was officially founded, driven by the dedication of Patsy Tobin and Paddy Doherty.

2013

Patsy Tobin and Paddy Doherty received awards for their long-standing commitment to handball in South Tipperary.

Present

The club continues to foster sporting excellence, community engagement, and competition success at county, provincial, and national levels.

Club Achievements

Juvenile Training & Achievements

Our juvenile training sessions are held every Wednesday from 6pm to 7pm and are led by David Tobin. The program welcomes boys and girls of all ages, providing gloves, goggles, and a ball for each participant. New members are always welcome to join and experience the sport in a supportive environment.

Notable Juvenile Achievements:
  • Stefan Tobin – Winner of the school Munster Handball competition (2017), representing Gaelscoil and Carrick-on-Suir Handball Club; went on to represent Munster in schools handball finals. Won the U11 school singles county final (2017) and U10 Tipperary 40x20 county final (2017), as well as the U10 60x30 county final (2016).
  • Tadgh Quinlan – Played in the U12 40x20 Tipperary county final (2017) and U11 60x30 county final (2016).
  • Henry Kirby & Rian Power – Competed in U10 doubles Tipperary county 40x20 final (2017).
  • Stefan Tobin & Tadgh Quinlan – Winners of South Tipperary finals doubles in U10 and U11 60x30 handball (2016).

Adult Achievements

  • Jason Tobin – All-Ireland Junior B 40x20 singles champion (2003) and participant in World Handball Final (2003).
  • Derek Tobin & David Butler – U15 doubles All-Ireland champions (1995).
  • David Tobin – Over-40 doubles Munster champion (2012), All-Ireland 60x30 Masters B Singles champion (2015), and doubles All-Ireland winner with Eddie Farrell (2015).
  • Patsy Tobin – Former Munster titleholder in his younger years.
  • Stephen Moroney – Team of Six All-Ireland U16/17 winner (2011).

Additionally, the club has amassed numerous Tipperary county 40x20 final medals over the years, achieved by members including Paul Tobin, Christopher McGrath, Gerard Tobin, Paul Casey, and many others mentioned above. These successes have collectively contributed to the club's All-Ireland and Munster titles.
