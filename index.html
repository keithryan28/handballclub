About Handball Club

Founded in 1978, Handball Club has grown to become one of the most respected handball clubs in the region.

We're dedicated to promoting the sport of handball and racquetball through excellent facilities, professional coaching, and competitive leagues. Our club welcomes players of all ages and skill levels.
