Planning Permission Application

Dec 12, 2025


Posted on Dec 12, 2025

Ambitious Times Ahead

The Handball Club is seeking planning permission to extend its existing single-court facility to a modern, three-court complex that will better serve the growing membership and the wider community. The proposed development includes two additional handball halls and an upper-floor extension featuring a viewing area, meeting space, and kitchen facilities. This upgrade will provide improved opportunities for training, competition, and community events, ensuring the club can continue to promote handball and support local sporting activity for many years to come.
