Join Carrick-on-Suir Handball Club

Enjoy full access to our courts, gym, and community events. Membership opens the door to online court bookings and exclusive club activities.

View Membership Prices Book a Court

Membership Pricing

Membership renewals occur each September. Join now to gain access to all club facilities and the online booking system.

Adult Membership

€50 per year. Full access to courts, gym, and club facilities. Pay-for-lights applies for court and gym use.

Juvenile Membership

€20 per year. Access to courts, gym, and all youth training programs.

Family Membership

€60 per year. Covers two adults and any number of juveniles in one household.

Upon joining, all members are added to the online booking system, allowing you to reserve courts conveniently from home.

Booking a Court Online

Members can book courts online using the TeamUP app. This system ensures fair access for all members and allows you to:

  • Check court availability in real-time
  • Reserve your preferred time slot
  • Manage your personal bookings easily

Simply log in with your membership details to access the booking system. Instructions will be provided upon joining.
